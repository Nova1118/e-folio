Philippine Association of Students in Office Administration

The official student body who represents the entire students of Bachelor of Science in Office Administration (BSOA).

My key role as Organization President of PASOA ParSU Chapter is to lead the organization by presiding over different meetings either organization meetings or representing campus wide organizations meetings, overseeing major decisions, maintaining communications with the members and advisors of the organization and other student body of the university, and ensures that the organization operates, functions and do its responsibilities through conducting different activities for its members development of their leadership skills, enhancing teamwork abilities, building a social network, boosting confidence, and fostering a sense of community within the campus, while contributing to personal growth and career development and joining activities hosted by other HEI’s.
