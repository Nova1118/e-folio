Youth Volunteer Formation Course (YVFC)

Undergoes foundational training program offered by the Philippine Red Cross Youth (RCY) aimed at educating and empowering young individuals, aged 7 to 25, in the principles and humanitarian values of the Red Cross Movement.

Core Modules of YVFC includes:

  • Youth Volunteer Orientation Module

    • Introduces participants to the history, mission, and seven fundamental principles of the Red Cross—Humanity, Impartiality, Neutrality, Independence, Voluntary Service, Unity, and Universality. It also covers the significance of the Red Cross emblem and the organization's various services.

  • Values Formation Course

    • Provides a platform for self-awareness and reflection, encouraging participants to assess their personal values and beliefs. This module fosters the development of leadership qualities aligned with the humanitarian ideals of the Red Cross.

  • Team Development

    • Focuses on building leadership skills and understanding group dynamics. Through workshops and interactive activities, participants learn about team building, organizational development, and effective leadership strategies.
