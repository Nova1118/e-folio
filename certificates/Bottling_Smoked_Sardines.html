Bottling Smoked Sardines

Attended the second episode of BCAARRD's Online Technology Dissemination and Training, featuring the topic “Bottling Smoked Sardines,” presented by Prof. Myrna C. Bigueja of Partido State University – Sagnay Campus, held on June 7, 2022 via Zoom and Facebook livestream. The session discussed the thermal processing of smoked sardines using bottles, emphasizing their high nutritional value and extended shelf life. The bottled product is rich in essential minerals such as sodium, calcium, potassium, iron, and zinc, making it a nutritious and sustainable food option.
