Edutech Caravan CBA Annual Business Summit 2023: “Accounting and Business Landscape in the New Normal: A Road to Resilience, Recovery and Reinventions”

CBM joins the 2023 Annual Business Summit with the theme, "Accounting and Business Landscape in the New Normal: A Road to Resilience, Recovery, and Reinvention," the Partido State University College of Business and Management participated in the Business Summit 2023 hosted by the Ateneo de Naga University College of Business and Accountancy on March 29–31, 2023, at the Arrupe Convention Hall.

Aside from ParSU, different Higher Education Institutions (HEIs) across the Bicol Region also attended the said summit. The business summit features different seminar workshops with topics such as business, logistics, marketing, heritage tourism, accounting and finance, and digital technology.

Along with the summit, a trade fair was also held at Xavier Hall, which allowed student entrepreneurs from ADNU and other HEIs, including ParSU, to showcase their products and enterprises. The said trade fair is available until Friday, March 31, 2023.
