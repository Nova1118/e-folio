OPISINNA (Office Environment Protection and Safety Capacity Development in the New Normal)

The objective of the webinar is to launch the OPISINNA Extension Program and to capacitate office workers with the knowledge and skills they need to keep fit, safe, and secure while maintaining a wholesome working environment throughout the pandemic and beyond.

OPISINNA (Office Environment Protection and Safety Capacity Development in the New Normal)

Participated in the launching of the extension project “Opportunities and Empowering Women and Men Individuals and Office Staff in the New Normal Agenda (O.P.I.S.I.N.N.A),” organized by the BS in Office Administration program of the Partido State University – College of Business and Management, in partnership with the University’s Extension & Community Services. The event featured a webinar titled “Office Environment Protection and Safety Capacity Development in the New Normal,” held on October 8, 2021. Topics covered included workplace safety and environmental protection, presented by Engr. Kier P. Gasga (“Keeping Yourself Safe in Your Own Workplace”) and Engr. Emelina R. Padayao (“Office Environment Protection and Safety”).
